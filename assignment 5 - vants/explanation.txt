I tried to make something kinda like Splatoon where the different vants fought to color the ground with their color.

Red vants:
Heavy on conversion. When they find a pheromone that isn't theirs, they convert it to their pheromone. They only turn
when they find a pheromone that is empty, and they turn to the left

Green vants:
They love to fill in areas. They mostly stick to their own turf, slowly converting the spaces next to their pheromones
into their own. If they are in their own pheromone, they keep going in the same direction and don't eat their own pheromone.
When they find a different pheromone, they convert it and turn left or right depending on if they found nothing, red, or blue.

Blue vants:
They love to eat the other pheromones. Whenever they find a pheromone, they eat it and leave behind nothing. When they find nothing,
they leave behind their own pheromone. They only turn when they run into their own pheromone to avoid eating all the pheromones
the other blue vants have left behind.

The vants have different strategies. The blue vants like to get rid of everything and turn nothing into their own area, avoiding
the blue pheromone to prevent accidentally erasing what a fellow blue vant has done. The green vants slowly expand their areas,
focusing on maintaining the spaces they already occupy. They also don't eat their own pheromone. The red vants are kind of the chaos
vant. They eat their pheromone and replace all other pheromones. They turn erratically and have the weirdest patterns of the three.
